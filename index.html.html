About Rolling Sushi Truck

Rolling Sushi Truck is a student-focused food truck concept created for the UAH community. Our goal is to offer convenient meals that are affordable, visually appealing, and easy to grab between classes.

Cooked-Only Menu

Every item is fully cooked, making the menu more accessible and comfortable for a wider range of customers.

Affordable Pricing

Designed with college students in mind, with meal options aimed at realistic campus-friendly prices.

Fast Service

Quick ordering and easy grab-and-go meals so students can eat without missing class or work.
